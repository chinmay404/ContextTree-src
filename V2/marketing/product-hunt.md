# Product Hunt listing — ready to submit

**Name:** ContextTree
**Tagline (60):** The AI chat studio for people who think in branches
**Launch day:** Tuesday or Wednesday, 00:01 PT

**Description (260):**
Every AI conversation becomes a tree. Ask side questions without polluting
your main thread, continue any branch on another model (context comes
along), compare branches side by side and promote the winner. Your keys,
your data, exportable always.

**Topics:** Artificial Intelligence, Productivity, SaaS

Hey PH 👋 I built ContextTree because my best AI threads kept dying from

branches. Three things I'd love your take on: (1) new canvases start as a
